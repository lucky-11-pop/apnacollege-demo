About Our School

Founded in 1995, Springfield International School has been a pioneer in modern education. We believe in nurturing young minds to become global leaders of tomorrow by providing a balanced environment of academics, sports, and arts.

Our Core Values

  • Innovation: Encouraging creative thinking.
  • Inclusivity: Welcoming students from all walks of life.
  • Respect: Fostering a polite and kind community.
